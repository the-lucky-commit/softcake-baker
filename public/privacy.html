Privacy Policy for Apple Bake Bot

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Apple Bake ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our Facebook Messenger automated bot.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with our Messenger bot, we may receive basic public profile information provided by Facebook (such as your name and profile picture) and any messages or order details you voluntarily share with us to process your cake orders.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries via Facebook Messenger.
  • Process and fulfill your orders.
  • Improve our customer service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We share data with third parties only as necessary to provide our services (such as delivery partners) or as required by law.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Facebook Page or WhatsApp.
