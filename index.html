❄️ Storage Instructions

Shelf life: 5–7 days
Keep refrigerated at 1–5°C

‼️ Soft cakes MUST be refrigerated!
Without refrigeration → may spoil within 1 day

📋 Tips:
1️⃣ Refrigerate immediately upon receiving
2️⃣ If selling without a fridge, use styrofoam box with cooling gel or dry ice
3️⃣ After removing from fridge → best consumed within 24 hours
4️⃣ If re-refrigerated → can last up to 2 more days
